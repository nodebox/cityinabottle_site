"City" goes to Denmark

Last week, we were invited to participate in an international arts expo in Denmark.  So we loaded up the car and drove whole the way up to the north.  It took us 12 hours!

Turnout was better than expected and we sure generated a lot of interest.  We met some really sweet and interesting people there.  But more importantly, we now have our presentation fixed and ready.  So if ever we need to introduce ourselves, the work is done!


- Our setup.  We laid the carpet ourselves!  And that seat next to the beamer?  Very expensive!  Very!


- These guys came from the other side of Denmark just to hear me yack on about insects and plants scurrying around for food.
