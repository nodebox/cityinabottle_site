E-Culture Fair 2010, Dortmund

From August 23 to 25, the E-Culture Fair 2010 will be showcasing innovative projects in the fields of creative industry, research, education and media art at the Dortmunder U.

ECF will take place during ISEA (International Symposium for Electronic Arts) in Dortmund, Germany. We will exhibit interactive installations at ECF with City In A Bottle, NodeBox and Dark Matter (partner project, Tom Heene, Pieter Heremans et al.)
