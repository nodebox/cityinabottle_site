What if our insects were weapons?

Funny.  I've stumbled across a game that uses the same principles of emergent behaviour, but targeted at one single item of gameplay, namely weapons.

Galactic Arms Race is a multiplayer spaceshooter.  Nothing special there, until you come to the weapons section.  The lasers have not been designed, but are generated by the AI.  And it provides creative solutions for killing enemies!  Check it out:
